How to Check NCVT ITI Result and download ITI Marksheet Certificate?

For students who have participated in the NCVT ITI exam and want to check their NCVT MIS Result, first of all, I should advise you to note down your roll and registration number from your NCVT ITI Admit Card. Then, go to the official website of NCVT/New Skill India portal, Click on the “Marksheet Verification” link, enter your roll or registration numbers, and Click the Verify button. Now, you can view your result on the screen and also download your NCVT ITI Marksheet and ITI Certificate.

Here are step-by-step guides to check NCVT MIS ITI Results through the NCVT portal.

  • Visit the NCVT MIS official website: www.ncvtmis.gov.in.
  • Then, Click on the “Marksheet Verification” link.
  • Enter your roll or registration numbers and other required details.
  • Click the “Verify” button.
  • Now, you can see your ITI result on the screen.
  • Then you can download your trainee mark sheet Certificate.

Note: If the server of the official website of NCVT goes down, then you can check late night or early morning as the website works better at that time.

Step-by-step guide to download your ITI Result and ITI Marksheet through Skill India Portal.

I want to tell all 1st and 2nd-year ITI students that the Government of India has launched a new Skill India Portal at www.skillindiadigital.gov.in, so now you all can check your results from this new SIDH Portal too; just follow all the steps given below.

Step 1- First of all, Candidates have to visit the SIDH Portal, www.skillindiadigital.gov.in.

Step 2- After that, you have to go to the login section and login by entering your login details like username and password..

Step 3- Now, three options will appear on your screen: ‘Learner’, ‘Partner’, and ‘ITI Trainee Result 2024’. 1st and 2nd year candidates must choose the third option.

Step 4- After selecting the ITI Trainee Result 2024 option, a new page will open on your screen, where you have to submit information like your Roll Number/PRN Number, and Date of birth.

Step 5- After clicking the submit button, a new dashboard will open on your screen, then click the three-dot menu on the top right of this dashboard, Now, you will see your trainee details, like- viewing results, download mark sheet, download certificate, etc.

Step 6- Then, tap the ‘View Result’ option for checking your NCVT ITI Result.

Step 7- Now, you can view your ITI Result and also download your Original ITI Marksheet and ITI Certificate.

NCVT: A brief overview

Under the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, the Indian Government provides counseling services through the National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). The National Council of Vocational Training was created by the Government of India in 1956. During June or December Every year, The National Council of Vocational Training conducts exams for 1st and 2nd-year students. 

The NCVT Provides students with short- and long-term courses like Fitter, Electrician, Electronics Mechanic, Mechanic Diesel, Instrument Mechanic, Civil Engineer Assistant, Architectural Assistant, Plumber, Welder, etc. Eligibility criteria Students who pass 10th or 12th Class exams can enroll in these courses.

The duration of these ITI courses varies from 6 months to 2 years. Students can be admitted to these courses based on their interests. The benefit of these ITI courses is that the students gain practical technical knowledge. After doing ITI courses, you can apply for vacancies in different sectors like railway, defense, private, etc., to get a job.

What is ITI, and how does the ITI Training Center work? 

The government of India has established Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs). ITIs are training centers with institutes spread all over India. These institutes offered many courses, both engineering and non-engineering. 

The specialty of an ITI course is that it provides more practical training than theory so that the student can understand it well. 8th, 10th, or 12th-class students can apply for these courses. If you want to get a job quickly, an ITI course is best for you. After completing the ITI trade course, you can do an apprenticeship which will help you get a better job

ITI training center teaches you practical skills like fixing electrical problems, repairing cars, and using computers. The teacher teaches you these skills practically. By learning these skills properly, students become job-ready.

How to get admission to ITI Colleges?

It is very easy to get admission to ITI Government colleges. The ITI form is expected to be released in June and July of every year. Firstly, the candidates must fill out the application form to be admitted to ITI College. You can apply the form from the official ITI website of the state from where you want to do the ITI course. 

The official website will release merit lists based on class 8th, 10th, and 12th students. After that, only the student whose name appears on the merit list will get a chance to sit in the counseling. After counseling, students can be admitted to ITI Government and private colleges in engineering and non-engineering trade as their interest.

What are the eligibility criteria to get admission in ITI college?

Most courses require a 10th or 12th pass to get admission to ITI courses. General criteria for admission to ITI courses in NCVT. Following this criteria are given below.

  • Students must have Indian citizenship.
  • For most ITI courses, 10th and 12th passes are required to get admission.
  • For some courses, an 8th pass is required to get admission.
  • Students’ age limit should be 14 to 40.
  • For some trades, candidates’ physical fitness is required.
  • For some courses, admission is required to take the entrance exam.
